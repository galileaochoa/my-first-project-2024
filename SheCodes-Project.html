Anya Taylor-Joy as Beth Harmon in The Queen's Gambit

"The Queen's Gambit" is a miniseries that follows the life of Beth Harmon, an orphaned chess prodigy, from her childhood
in an orphanage to her rise as a competitive chess player. Set in the 1950s and 1960s, the show explores Beth's journey as
she battles personal demons, addiction, and societal expectations. Despite facing challenges and gender biases in the
male-dominated world of chess, Beth becomes a formidable player and challenges the norms of the time. The series received
widespread acclaim for its compelling storytelling, strong performances, and its portrayal of the game of chess.
